Bot Info

DomCentral is a Discord bot I've developed to provide valuable assistance to users who may not have a keyholder or dominant.
Beyond being a coding hobby, this bot offers users a range of additional features and enhancements to enrich their experience.

Responsible Use Notice: Please be aware that any tasks and outcomes generated by the bot should be undertaken at your own discretion and risk.
It's essential to consider your own well-being and boundaries when engaging with the bot.
If a task or punishment is not suitable for you due to health concerns or personal reasons, do not feel obligated to complete it.
Your well-being and comfort are paramount.

Additionally, if you find that you lack the necessary items or toys to complete a task, you can use the command again to receive a different task that better suits your situation.
Your experience with the bot should always be enjoyable and respectful of your personal circumstances.

If you encounter any issues, discover bugs, or have further information to share about the bot, we encourage you to reach out to @GreenYellow on the ChastityMaze Discord server.
Your feedback helps us improve and maintain a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone.
